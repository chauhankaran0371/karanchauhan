About Us The Story of La Pino'z Pizza

The story of India's fastest growing pizza place started back in 2011 when Sanam Kapoor opened his very first pizzeria in his hometown Chandigarh. Inspired by his own search for delicious pizza options, he started to wonder if there was a better way of doing pizza – and business. The last thing the world needed was another pizzeria, but maybe this one could be different and everyone could get exactly what they wanted, made fresh on demand. It all started sounding pretty great, and soon Sanam was opening the first La Pino'z in his hometown Chandigarh and there was no looking back. As of today, La Pino'z has 500+ outlets across India with the recent one opening in London.
